**Patent Instructions**  
**As part of your project, you are required to submit a write up for a provisional patent. This will be a simple assignment as all you will need to do is write a few paragraphs about your project's concept and how your project works. All you need to do is to follow the directions as follows. We ask you a set of simple questions, and as long as you answer them you will hit the word minimum. Since this is a patent, speak formally. You may use the first person. You must refer to your project as your "invention" throughout the patent.**

**Name**  
The name of your provisional patent should be the name of your project, followed by a colon, then a small tagline to describe it. For example, for a project that manages a to-do list, its title would be "To-Do List: A Simple Yet Effective Method of Reminding People of Tasks to be Completed and Improving Everyday Life."

**Background of the Invention (150 words)**  
What kind of framework does your project use (eg Flutter)? Who is your invention intended for (you can name multiple groups of people)? What kinds of problems does your intended audience face everyday? Explain how this is a big problem that affects their local communities as well. Your invention will improve their quality of life, by doing specifically what?

**Summary of the Invention (150 words)**  
The significance of your invention is tied to the problem that it's trying to solve. State again the problem that your intended audience often faces. Specifically, how does your invention address this? What are other products on the market that do something similar to your own? How does your invention improve on those other products? Highlight the main features of your invention and explain its benefits.

**Detailed Description of the Invention (200 words)**  
Give a tour of your invention as if you were giving a demonstration of it. Start from your starting page. Then, explain any features of the app that the page utilizes (for example, a login screen, a post, a feed). How is a user supposed to interact with this screen? If there is another screen that is accessible, then move to that screen and repeat the process.

This is intended to be a short summary of everything you wrote. First, state the problem that your targeted audience often faces. Your solution is exactly what? What does your invention do? Highlight all its features. How does your invention solve this issue?
